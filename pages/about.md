I'm Dade. Solo founder and creative director of [Dade.Studio](https://dade.studio), a brand, marketing, and design agency for people who care about the work.

This blog is where I build in public. Right now that means three threads:

- [MaaS](https://memory.dade.studio), an AI Memory as a Service platform I'm shipping in beta.
- A Neocities revival project that doubles as a technical playground.
- Studio work, when there's something worth showing.

Expect build logs, essays, design notes, and writeups of the tools I actually use. No roadmaps, no thought-leader posturing, no lists of ten things you need to know. Just the work and what I'm learning from it.
