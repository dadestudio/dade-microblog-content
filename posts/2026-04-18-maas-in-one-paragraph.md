Every AI conversation starts from zero. You spent an hour last Tuesday explaining your business model, your tone, your customers, and the stack you actually use, and the agent you opened today has no idea any of that happened. So you re-explain. Then you re-explain again next week, in a different tool, to a different model. The context tax compounds and nobody sends you the bill.

MaaS fixes this with a single private memory file that any AI can read. You write your context once: who you are, what you build, how you talk, the projects in flight, the decisions you have already made. Cursor reads it. ChatGPT reads it. Claude reads it. Whatever ships next year reads it. The file lives on your terms, syncs across the tools you already use, and travels with you when you switch models.

Who this is for: solo founders, independent creators, and small studios who use AI every day and are tired of repeating themselves to a goldfish. If your work depends on continuity and you do not have time to babysit prompts, this is built for you.
